Privacy Policy - Scheme Master

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Scheme Master. This application is designed to help students and farmers discover government welfare schemes, understand eligibility criteria, and receive guidance on required documents. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • User category (Student or Farmer)

1.2 Document Information

  • Uploaded documents (such as Aadhaar, income certificate, caste certificate)
  • Document status (uploaded / verified / missing)

1.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction data
  • Device information
  • Log and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users securely
  • To display relevant student and farmer schemes
  • To suggest schemes based on uploaded documents
  • To validate document completeness
  • To guide users in obtaining missing documents
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. This includes secure database storage, restricted access, and encrypted communication. Scheme Master does not sell or misuse user data.

4. User Rights

  • View and update your profile information
  • Upload or remove documents
  • Request account deletion
  • Control how your data is used within the app

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps (for nearby government office guidance)
  • Email services (for password recovery)

We ensure that any third-party services used comply with data protection standards.

6. Children’s Privacy

Scheme Master is intended for general users including students above the eligible age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on this page.
